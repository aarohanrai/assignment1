Biography

"Darren Jason Watkins Jr. (born January 21, 2005), known online as IShowSpeed (or simply Speed), is an American online streamer and YouTuber. He is best known for his variety livestreams, in which he primarily plays video games, including FIFA, Fortnite, and Roblox."

ISHOWSPEED won his first Breakout Streamer of The Year back in 2022. It was the 12th Stream Awards show where all the famous streaner like KaiCenat were competing against eachother.
